Pre-work - Week 1

My first week was more than I expected. Having worked with git during the intro class, I felt like I would be able to breeze through. I learned way more than I expected especially how it is used within GitHub for collaboration. The HTML and CSS are about what I expected.

Honestly, right now it is time. I have 2 weeks of work and the prework was a little more time consuming than I initially expected. I just need to power through and wait until I can commit all my time to bootcamp.

At the time of this writing I am working through a challenge in CSS with the card exercise.

I am very excited about Javascript and SQL as I have seen what these languages can do first hand at my current place of employment.
